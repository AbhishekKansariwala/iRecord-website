Our Story

Our Story

iRecord was built to solve a problem the Indian capital market community had long accepted.

For decades, community relied on complex Excel sheets, manual entries, and even handwritten records to manage portfolios. Data was fragmented, calculations were time-intensive, and critical areas like capital gains reporting were often error-prone and difficult to track.

As portfolios scaled, so did the complexity but the tools didn't.

What began as an effort to streamline reporting evolved into a unified platform designed to replace scattered systems with structure and accuracy. By eliminating manual dependencies and standardizing workflows, iRecord brings consistency, transparency, and precision to portfolio management.

From real-time reporting to simplified capital gains and performance tracking, the platform is built to reduce friction and enable better decision-making.

Today, iRecord serves as the technology backbone for modern wealth professionals—transforming how data is managed, interpreted, and delivered.

Replacing complexity with clarity.

Founded in 2007, incorporated in 2017, today iRecord is widely used and time tested in the industry serving more than 75,000+ active demat holders.
